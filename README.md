## Blink example

1. The meaning of:

 * | : or bit to bit
 * & : and bit to bit
 * ^ : exclusive or
 * ~ : Complement to one or bit to bit negation
 * << : move to left
 * >> : move to right

| b | a | b or a | b and a | b xor a | not b |
